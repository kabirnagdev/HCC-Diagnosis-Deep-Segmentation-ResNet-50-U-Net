# Code of Conduct

This project adopts a professional, respectful, and inclusive collaboration standard.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on evidence-based technical discussion.
- Give and accept feedback in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private/sensitive information without consent.
- Any abusive, threatening, or hostile conduct.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when needed to protect community health.
